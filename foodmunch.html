About us

Cara Cara is more than a rooftop bar cafe ,it's a sanctuary in the heart of the city, merging natural elements with urban sophistication. From the fluid architecture to the serene spaces, every detail is designed to offer a unique, unforgettable experience.Founded in 2020, Cara Cara is a rooftop sanctuary perched above the city's bustle. Our name, inspired by the sweetest citrus, reflects our philosophy - vibrant flavors should be paired with breathtaking views
